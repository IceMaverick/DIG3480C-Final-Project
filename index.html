Wit's Castle Raid

Wit is your average, giant-haired dude with superpowers just trying to make his way in this world.
However, even the best of us find ourselves a little short on change now and again.
So, like any reasonable person would do, Wit set out on an adventure to plunder a castle
in hopes of finding something to pay for dinner tonight.





Use the Arrow Keys to Move.

Your character, Wit, can surge up walls
by holding jump while running into them.

Wit can also cling to ceilings
by holding the jump button.
